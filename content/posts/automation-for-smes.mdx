## Common pain points
Manual reporting, duplicate data entry, and slow approvals are the most frequent blockers.

## Quick wins
- Consolidate data sources into a single dashboard.
- Automate notifications and approvals.
- Replace spreadsheets with lightweight internal tools.

## Strategy
Start with one workflow, prove ROI, then expand to adjacent processes.
